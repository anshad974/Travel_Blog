Exploring Ubud Temples

By Admin • July 12, 2025

Ubud, in the heart of Bali, is known for its lush rice terraces, spiritual culture, and stunning temples. On my recent trip, I explored hidden shrines surrounded by tropical greenery, where locals perform daily rituals.

The famous Goa Gajah (Elephant Cave) dates back to the 9th century and features intricate stone carvings. Another must-see is the Tirta Empul Temple, where Balinese Hindus take part in a holy water cleansing ritual.

If you’re looking for a peaceful escape from Bali’s beaches, the temples around Ubud offer a glimpse into the island’s rich history and spirituality.
