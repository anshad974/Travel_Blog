Trekking in Patagonia

By Sarah • August 5, 2025

Patagonia, the southernmost region of South America, is a land of glaciers, windswept plains, and dramatic mountain ranges. Trekking here is unlike anywhere else in the world — vast open spaces and towering peaks await the adventurous traveler.

The most famous trek is the “W” circuit in Torres del Paine National Park, where you’ll witness emerald lakes, granite towers, and glaciers. Trails are well-marked, and refugios (mountain lodges) make it possible to trek without carrying a tent.

For those seeking more solitude, the Fitz Roy range in Argentina offers challenging hikes with rewarding views of jagged peaks and turquoise lakes. Be prepared for unpredictable weather and strong winds — they’re part of the Patagonia experience!

Trekking in Patagonia requires planning, but the reward is an unforgettable adventure at the edge of the world.
